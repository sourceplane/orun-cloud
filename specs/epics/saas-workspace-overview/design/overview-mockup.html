lumen is a production SaaS baseline — fork it, rebrand in one script, and grow it a few components at a time. Every Worker, Terraform stack, and migration declares its own component.yaml; the repo as a whole is platform intent.

What this workspace manages

The lumen platform: a multi-tenant SaaS control plane on Cloudflare Workers + Supabase. CI never runs a raw pnpm, wrangler, or terraform — it runs orun plan and orun run, compiling the platform into deterministic state and converging deviation on every commit.

Bounded contexts

  • Identity, organizations & membership, projects/environments
  • RBAC & policy, audit & observability
  • Metering, billing (Polar), webhooks, notifications

Important information

  • Runtime is live on stage and prod via orun run.
  • Data plane provisioned by Terraform; credentials in AWS Secrets Manager.
  • Migrations run through the db-migrate component (plan on PRs, apply on merge).
